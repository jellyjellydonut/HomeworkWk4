About me

Hi! My name is Demi and I am an ecological restoration professional. I work for the Stewardship Team at NYC Parks where I lead volunteers in restoration projects in natural areas city-wide. I'm stoked and grateful to be a part of The Urban Wild's 4th cohort of the Intro to AgTech Program. I look forward to sharing what I grow with friends, neighbors, and community fridges. With growing climate uncertainties and disruptions to food supply, it's crucial to be armed with the knowledge on how to grow your own food.

Written by Demi Amideneau, student of The Urban Wild
